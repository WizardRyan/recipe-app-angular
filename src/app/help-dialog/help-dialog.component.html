Help

What is this?

This is a web app for you to post your favorite recipes and view recipes others have made.

How can I post a recipe?

First, either create an account or sign in. Then, head over to the "Add" tab and fill out the form there.

How can I delete a recipe?

If you posted the recipe, you can click the garbage bin on the recipes page to delete. If a recipe has been flagged more than once, anyone is now allowed to delete that recipe.

How do I post a comment?

Sign in, then click on either the title or image of a recipe to view the recipe details, then scroll down to post a comment

How can I delete a comment?

The poster of the recipe, and the poster of the comment are both allowed to delete a given comment.
